Welcome!

Rêve: the Dream Ouroboros is a French fantasy tabletop role-playing game created by Denis Gerfaud. It is the translation of Rêve de Dragon, a best-selling game in France.

This website is unoffical and fan-made. Its purpose is to promote Rêve de Dragon in the English world, and to offer resources for the game so that more people may play it.

Some of the key features that define Rêve:

  • Dreamworlds: Rêve: the Dream Ouroboros images that all worlds and everything in them (including your own character!) are dreamt by an inaccessible race of dragons.
  • Any Genre: Whilst the author of Rêve primarily dreamt fantasy worlds, the game does allow game masters to create any world, of any genre, in any era. Within the game, characters may even travel between genres, and through time, via Grey Dreams, Dream Rifts, and Blur Dreams.
  • Story-Driven: Rêve is first and foremost a game about experiencing epic imaginary stories. Any world or story that the game master can dream of can be played.
  • Humour: Rêve is a serious game that doesn't take itself too seriously. Especially when magic goes wrong... then anything, funny or horrific, is possible.
  • Archetype Quest: In Rêve, characters don't die. They re-awaken, in another dream, with new skills and characteristics. They remember their previous lives like somebody trying to remember the dreams they had the night before. Except, when their previous lives, their Archetype, calls to them...
  • Destiny-Driven: Characters who follow their destiny earn Destiny Points that allows them to perform tasks otherwise inaccessible or chimerically difficult. This game mechanic, helps ensure that players follow the scenario and helps create truly epic moments late in the game.
